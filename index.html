Words - A Compendium of their Past and Present

Words tell stories - stories of their inception, their journey, places they went, forms they took.

They take birth from the pale shadows of the forgotten. They make friendships; they fight battles. They are the companions of one's solitude; they are the hovering laughter over late night chatters.

And when the period of their sojourn is over, they slowly wither away into obsolescence, only to be remembered in reflections.

Verbum is about words - how they were and how they are. Type in a word and discover its existence in the past and the present.
